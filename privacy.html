Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TAVS Commerce Studio handles information when an authorized user uses the service.

1. Information processed

The service may process account identifiers, authorization status, content files, product information, captions, publishing settings, workflow records, validation results, and technical logs required to operate the service.

2. TikTok account information

When a user connects a TikTok account through an authorized OAuth process, the service may receive information permitted by the user and the approved TikTok scopes. The service does not request or store the user's TikTok password.

3. How information is used

Information is used to authenticate authorized users, prepare and validate content, manage approval workflows, track publishing status, prevent duplicate actions, maintain security, and diagnose technical problems.

4. Local storage and security

TAVS Commerce Studio is designed primarily as a local workflow tool. Sensitive authorization data may be stored using operating-system security features. Reasonable technical safeguards are used to reduce unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss.

5. Sharing of information

Information is not sold to advertisers. Information may be shared with third-party platforms only when necessary to perform an action requested and authorized by the user, or when required by law.

6. Third-party services

TikTok and other connected services operate under their own privacy policies and technical requirements. Users should review the privacy practices of each connected platform.

7. Data retention

Workflow records and local files may be retained while needed for operation, audit, recovery, security, or compliance. Users may remove local project data, disconnect an account, or revoke authorization through the relevant platform settings.

8. User choices

Users may choose not to connect a third-party account, may revoke authorization, and may stop using the service. Revoking access may prevent connected features from functioning.

9. Children's privacy

The service is not intended for children below the minimum age required to use the applicable third-party platforms or to enter legally binding agreements.

10. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the service, applicable laws, or third-party platform requirements change. The latest version will be published on this page.

11. Contact

Privacy questions may be submitted through the official contact channel provided by the owner of TAVS Commerce Studio.
